# Embase Research Skill

Search comprehensive biomedical literature from Embase - the leading European database with extensive pharmaceutical and clinical trial coverage.

## Capabilities

- **Search Embase Database**: Query journals, conference proceedings, drug information
- **Filter by Subject**: Medical specialties, drugs, devices, adverse events
- **Fetch Article Details**: Abstracts, indexing terms, author information
- **Track Drug Safety**: Pharmacovigilance and adverse reaction monitoring
- **Literature Analysis**: Support for systematic reviews and meta-analyses

## Usage Examples

### Search for literature
```
"Search Embase for: COVID-19 treatment"
"Find articles on immunotherapy in cancer"
"Search for drug safety studies about aspirin"
"Get conference proceedings on cardiology 2024"
```

### Drug safety and pharmacology
```
"Search Embase for: adverse reactions of [drug name]"
"Find pharmacokinetic studies"
"Search drug-drug interactions"
"Monitor new drug safety alerts"
```

### Systematic review support
```
"Search Embase with filters for systematic review"
"Find all RCTs on diabetes treatment"
"Get articles with specific MeSH terms"
"Search for articles published in 2023-2024"
```

## Data Source

- **Embase** (embase.com)
- Elsevier's biomedical database
- 32+ million records
- 7,600+ journals
- Conference proceedings from 1,900+ conferences
- Strong drug and pharmacology coverage

## Advantages Over PubMed

- Better European journal coverage
- More conference abstracts
- Superior drug and pharmacology indexing
- Extensive indexing of drug adverse reactions
- Covers more MEDLINE-indexed journals

## Notes

- Requires institutional subscription
- API access may require additional licensing
- Ovid and API interfaces available
- Excellent for systematic reviews with MEDLINE
